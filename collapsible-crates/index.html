Why Collapsible PP Crates?

Traditional wood or cardboard boxes take up full volume on the return trip. Collapsible polypropylene crates fold to roughly 20% of their assembled height, letting you stack 5x as many empties on a single pallet. That's real money on return logistics.

  • Folds flat in seconds — no tools required
  • Snap-lock corners hold under 1,200 lb dynamic load
  • GMA 48×40 in pallet-compatible footprints available
  • 100% waterproof — hoseable between cycles
