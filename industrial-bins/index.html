Why PP Over Other Materials

In industrial environments, polypropylene beats every alternative:

  • vs Wood: No splinters, no pest harborage, USDA-accepted for food facilities
  • vs Metal: No rust, no corrosion, fraction of the weight
  • vs HDPE totes: PP has better chemical resistance, lighter per cubic foot
  • vs Cardboard: Waterproof, reusable, no collapse under humidity
